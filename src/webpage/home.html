Welcome to Fermi

Fermi is a Spacebar-compatible client seeking to be as good as it can be with many features including:

  • Direct Messaging
  • Reactions support
  • Invites
  • Account switching
  • User settings
  • Developer portal
  • Bot invites
  • Translation support
